You can find all the best things to do right here in Ahmedabad, from concerts and cultural events to Indian history, free attractions and so much more.

It’s easy to ride the Metro or hop on a bike and explore all that the State’s capital has to offer. Walk the halls of free Calico museums, paddle on the sabarmati riverfront or sit back on a double-decker tour bus and soak up some history. Local shops, funky marketplaces and people-watching hot spots beckon by midday, as nighttime gives way to multi-course dinners and drinks from local breweries. However you spend your time here, make it count.

Catch all the live National and International cricket matches on world's biggest stadium

A testament to India's love for cricket, Narendra Modi Stadium is the largest cricket stadium in the world. With a seating capacity of 132,000 spectators, the stadium is a marvel of modern engineering and design. Its state-of-the-art facilities and unique features make it a truly world-class venue.
